Our Story

Who are we?

Bake With Yumna is a self made catering business which is run and founded by myself; Yumna. I have always had a huge sweet tooth and naturally found myself trying out new dessert recipies and flavours. I started out by baking small goodies for my family for our special days. Seeing my siblings smile while taking a bite of my chocolate chip cookies or lemon tarts made me the happiest older sister. I realized that even on the toughest days, a sweet treat can really cheer someone up. So I started this catering business; with the hopes to cheer people up with my dessserts!



What is a "must-try" item on the menu?

The "Ferrero Chocolate Cake" is a favorite of many of our customers and a personal fave as well. The rich chocolate fudge syrup pairs extremely well is the fluffy and light chocolate cake interior. This cake is topped with Ferrero Rocher chocolates which adds a crunchy texture!

What is our specialty?

We offer customizable options for all types of desserts. If you have an image in mind or a reference picture from instagram, pintrest etc. We are willing to recreate any sweets. We also make desserts from various cultures, including indian sweets, Tres leches, Crêpes etc.

How does Pricing work?

We offer base prices for classic cakes and desserts which can be found in our "Menu" section. However, pricing varies depending on the customizations made. Thus, after you place an order, I will contact you with a detailed breakdown on the pricing and we can work towards a order which fits your budget!
